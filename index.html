About Me

Hello! My name is Nicole Arrigo. I am a graduate student at North Carolina State University, pursuing a Masters in Civil Engineering with a concentration in Coastal Engineering. I am driven by my passion and dedication to sustainability and resilience. Currently, I am contributing my efforts to the Coastal & Computational Hydraulics research team, striving for innovative solutions for the challenges confronting coastal communities. I was born in Jacksonville, Florida and grew up in Saint Augustine.

Research

My research contributes to the NCSU Coastal & Computational Hydraulics Research Team. My current work focuses on modernizing a spectral wave model within a coupled modeling framework.
